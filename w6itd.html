Eviction Portal

This project was about presenting a future scenario in 30 years and demonstrating an interactive prototype that transports a user into the scenario by making aspects of the scenario experiential. Our team defined the scenario together. To build a high-fidelity prototype, my teammate Jennifer did the job of formgiving; Ward did the hardware part; I did the software part.

Project Brief

A critical future scenario was developed where the users, their behaviors, motivations and activities were identified. The project ended with we using an interactive prototype to transport a user into the scenario by making aspects of the scenario experiential.

A Future World

Interactive Prototype

The EVICTION PORTAL is a device inside every resident's home that allows them to browse through the personal data of their peers, in order to decide who must leave for the good of the community. The categories of data we selected were: Health, Sexuality, Profession, Popularity, Eviction ranking and Belief.

The living room designed for the final exhibition was made to look old-fashioned, which provided contrast with the future scenario.
