Roader Runner

Road runner is a game of controlling a car on computer screen and in physical world as well. Players use arrow keys to move a car on computer, and at the same time, a robot car will move the same way on a real map.

Duration
  • Dec, 2015 – Jan, 2016
Team
  • Kaiwen Xu
  • Wenjun Zhou
Tools
  • Arduino, Processing
  • Illustrator

Design Idea

By using arrow keys the player can input 8 instructions. A virtual car on screen and a real robot car will move the same way according to the instructions. The computer screen displays instant motion and location of the car through the game interface.

Implementation

The game is composed of the hardware design of a robot car and the software design of controlling system. Instructions are sent to Arduino (robot car) through the Bluetooth serial port.
