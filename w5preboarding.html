Easy Preboarding

The project was carried out in a hackathon organized by IKEA in Delft. I, as a designer, teamed up with a marketer and two developers. We had the unique opportunity to be surrounded by the inner workings of IKEA and got a lot of inspiration from mentors who supported the event. To meet the challenge of creating new waves of learning and development techniques, our team came up with the idea to provide a preboarding journey as the first step to start with the online learning platform for employees in IKEA.

IKODE Challenge

The big theme of the hackthon challenge is to make learning happen through radical innovation.

Design Brief

Due to the big scope of the challenge in hackathon, we focused our direction on learning skills at the same time as carrying out the work role. After talking with mentors form IKEA Competence Development management team and HR & Competence, we thought it valuable to introduce new technology to optimize onboarding experience and foster the future of learning.

Background Research

There is rarely connection between new hires and IKEA before the onboarding day. It could be a long and quiet period since the acceptance of an offer till the start of new career.

A study conducted by BambooHR in 2014 shares some valuable insights about onboarding. It collected responses from 1,005 individuals who were currently employed, over the age of 24, and located in the U.S.
